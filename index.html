About Me

Patel Mohit

AI/ML Engineer & Automation Specialist

Hello! I'm Mohit, a passionate AI/ML enthusiast and automation specialist currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Engineering at Government Engineering College, Bhavnagar (2022-2026).

My journey in technology is driven by a deep fascination with Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and automation. I've had the opportunity to work as an AI Agent Intern at CSRBoX, where I built mini AI agent projects and designed end-to-end AI workflows.

I specialize in building intelligent systems - from Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines using Whisper and Vector Databases, to large-scale Selenium automation for web scraping and data extraction. My projects have involved processing video content for semantic search, extracting datasets from platforms like Freepik and Pinterest, and developing ML models for predictive analysis.

My technical toolkit includes Python, Selenium, BeautifulSoup, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, and experience with LLM APIs like Gemini. I'm constantly exploring new frontiers in Deep Learning, NLP, and AI Agents.

I believe in building solutions that are not just technically sound but also practically impactful. Whether it's automating repetitive tasks or creating intelligent systems that understand and process information, I'm always excited to take on new challenges.
